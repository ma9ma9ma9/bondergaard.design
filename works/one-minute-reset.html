Overview

One Minute Reset is a breathing tool that does exactly one thing: helps you calm down in 60 seconds.

Most breathing apps are cluttered with features. I wanted something simpler. Click "Begin," follow the expanding circle, and breathe. 4 seconds in, 8 seconds out. That's it.

I built it with vanilla JavaScript and CSS animations. The circle moves with organic, fluid motion. Particles float across gradients that shift between warm amber and cool blue. Everything was designed to feel calming.
