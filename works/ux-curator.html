Overview

Staying updated on what's happening in design used to mean visiting a bunch of different sites and sifting through a lot of stuff that wasn't relevant. So I came up with UX Curator, an automated digest built around sources I selected and a filtering prompt I wrote myself.

UX Curator pulls from RSS feeds, scores articles by relevance, and sends me a curated digest three times a week. The AI handles the filtering, I just read what makes the cut. It covers the stuff I actually care about—product design and HCI—and explains why each article is relevant to my current role.
